About us

Our vision is to create and sustain a widely recognized academia in the brightest and the most promising students and researchers from all over the world, regardless of their social, economic or cultural background which can flourish and realize their overall potentiality. Our goal is to convert this college into an independent university in the future and produce visionary, tolerant, skilled and result-oriented human resources that can address the challenges of the 21st century. We are dedicated to develop Morgan into a leading academic institution of our country. Morgan International College is committed to provide quality education and to extend research based practical knowledge to the students.


Morgan offers the academically sound learning environment using high-tech teaching equipments. It has highly qualified and experienced faculty members. Morgan provides student-centered learning and teaching environment. Teaching is delivered by using modern technologies such as power point, web-surfing, audio-visuals etc. Morgan focuses on learning by doing whereby students actively participate in workshops, seminars, group discussions and presentations through power point.
